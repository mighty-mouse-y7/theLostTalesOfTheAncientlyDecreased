The Lost Tales of the Anciently Decreased

Introduction

Welcome friends, fiends, tourists, and travelers alike to The Lost Tales of the Anciently Decreased. I am the Dungeon Master(DM) of this fan made multiverse based on a combination of Dungeons and Dragons rules as well as homebrew rules.

Dragnor

Introduction

In the multiverse of The Lost Tales of the Anciently Decreased, Dragnor is canonically known as the birthplace of dragon kind. It is also the birth place of the dreaded tarrasque; however, as Dragnor is the birthplace of dragons, it is also home to the primordial dragons whoes favorite prey are the tarrasque.

Keltablique

Welcome to the dukedom of Keltablique. The first Duke Keltablique obtained his position in nobility by being the first to not onlu single-handedly kill a tarrasque in its own territory, but to also fend a horde of tarrasque from destroying his encampment. Today, the dukedom of Keltablique has grown to the large territory you see in the map above.

The walls are the height of three tarrasques standing atop one another and as thick as three tarrasques standing abreast.
